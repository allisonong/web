In which Allison tries to become a super-fit music critic by listening to every album on Rolling Stone's List of 500 Greatest Albums of All Time, while running.

ABOUT

When COVID 19 blew up, I was hoping to get into shape by training for a half marathon. Of course, the race was cancelled. Everything was cancelled and my motivation tanked. I started spending my whole day at home, either working at my desk or playing video games on the couch. By August I noticed that I was getting out of breath walking up stairs and my body had become squishier. I came up with this project force myself to go outside, get in aerobic shape, and develop a habit of exercising. Learning how to make a website (I am writing this website!) and becoming a culture snob are the carrot on the stick.
