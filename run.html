Hi! I'm Allison!

In 2020 I became a couch potato. I decided to get back in shape by running and listening to all the albums on Rolling Stones magazines list of the 500 Greatest Albums of all Time. I also decided to learn to code and make a website where I write about the music and my running. Welcome!

More About This Project

About

When the Coronavirus Pandemic hit Seattle in March 2020, I was training for a half marathon and looking forward to a summer of outdoor adventures. Of course, my race was cancelled, as were many of the outdoor adventures. Thus began a year of relative isolation and living and working from the same desk. It was an emotional rollercoaster, but one of the best things about 2020 was the new music I discovered and the way that music calmed me and gave me back the excitement that was missing in most parts of my life. In October I decided to use music to motivate myself to exercise and go outside again. I love music, and I hate running. But running is the cheapest, easiest, and most efficient way to get in shape. I also like learning new skills, so I decided I would learn to code and write a website myself. So, this project is a resolution to keep listening to new music, get in shape, and learn a new skill.

Rules

  1. I will listen to one album on the list per run.
  2. The first time I listen to an album must be on a run. No pre-listening!
  3. After each run I'll document the run and my thoughts/experience.
  4. I won't copy code for this website
