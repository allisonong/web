In 2020 I became a couch potato. I decided to get back in shape by running and listening to all the albums on Rolling Stones magazines list of the 500 Greatest Albums of all Time. I also decided to learn to code and make a website where I write about the music and my running. Welcome!

More About This Project

About

When the Coronavirus Pandemic hit Seattle in March 2020, I was training for a half marathon and looking forward to a summer of outdoor adventures. Of course, my race was cancelled, as were many of the outdoor adventures. Thus began a year of relative isolation and living and working from the same desk. It was an emotional dumpster fire (as many have said) but one of the best things about 2020 was the new music I discovered and the way that music calmed me and gave me back the excitement missing from the rest of my life. In October I decided to use music to motivate myself to exercise and go outside again. I love music, and I hate running. But running is the cheapest, easiest, and most efficient way to get in shape. I also like learning new skills, so I decided I would learn to code and write a website myself. So, this project is a resolution to keep listening to new music, get in shape, and learn a new skill.
